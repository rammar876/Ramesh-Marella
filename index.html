MCGR Framework

Multi-Cloud Governance & Reliability Model

The MCGR Model is a practical framework that integrates cloud governance, observability, cost optimization, and disaster recovery into a continuous feedback-driven operating model for enterprise multi-cloud environments.

1. Governance & Policy

IAM, RBAC, Zero Trust, compliance controls, policy-as-code, and operating standards.

2. Observability & SRE

SLOs, SLIs, monitoring, incident response, automation, and performance visibility.

3. Cost Optimization

Rightsizing, reserved capacity modeling, utilization analytics, and FinOps reporting.

4. DR & Resilience

RTO/RPO planning, multi-region readiness, failover validation, and resilience testing.
